About Us

"Viswateja" The School To Create Talented & Skillful Minds

The Viswateja high school was established on 3rd of June,2009 by Sri. D.Vasudeva Reddy which was an outstanding dream of him to develop English Medium Education in this rural area, with an attractive slogan "A Way To New Generation".

Our " Viswateja's " Mission and Vision

The aim of our school is to develop education teaching moral values. We are dedicated to provide your family with administrative and academic oversight. Our Correspondent D. Vasudeva Reddy sir offers a variety of curricula that can be customized based on your child's grade level and academic progress.

We have consistently upheld our motto of "Empowering Minds, Shaping Futures." Our school is dedicated to nurturing intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, creativity, and character development.

Our mission is to foster a dynamic learning environment that encourages academic excellence, social responsibility, and personal growth. We aim to cultivate well-rounded individuals who are equipped with the skills and values necessary to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our vision is to be a leader in education by creating a vibrant and inclusive community of learners. We envision our students as global citizens who contribute positively to society and lead with integrity and compassion.

Our " Viswateja's " Academics

At Viswateja High School, academics form the bedrock of our commitment to excellence. Our curriculum is meticulously designed to ignite curiosity, foster critical thinking, and inspire a love for learning. With a team of dedicated educators, we create an environment that encourages students to explore, question, and grow academically.
We are successfully running Abacus from 2st to 5th classes. In the year 2018-19 we introduced prestigious IIT Programme from 6th to 9th classes which is linked with Hyderabad Target IIT having 600 schools in stream. We also running Olympiad from 3rd to 5th class IC sections for which many parents paying lakhs of fee in corporate schools.
Key Features of our Academic Program:

  • Experienced Faculty: Our passionate educators bring a wealth of experience and expertise to the classroom. They not only impart knowledge but also mentor students, guiding them on their educational journey.
  • Advanced Resources: Our state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped laboratories and a comprehensive library, provide students with the tools they need to explore subjects in-depth and conduct meaningful research.
  • Continuous Assessment: We believe in a continuous learning process. Regular assessments, projects, and interactive sessions allow us to gauge students' progress and provide timely feedback.
  • Preparation for the Future: Beyond exams, our focus is on preparing students for life beyond school. We equip them with skills like communication, collaboration, and adaptability, essential for success in the modern world.
